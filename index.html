Know about EdTube

We are an Ed-Tech start-up company transforming the world of education by powering educationalists with the digital tools and resources which help them become future-ready. Our industry-first unified platform enables educators and students to unravel their true potential across the country on a streamlined platform with our next-level technology. We have a first of its kind personalised learning algorithm to identify conceptual gaps in students in every class. And through cumulative analysis, we would be tracking the deviation of the learning curve and recommend content to the students. Every question in our assessment is tagged with the appropriate cognitive parameter by which we track and develop high order thinking skills.
